Healthcare Awareness Campaign ROI Analysis (Diabetes Campaign)

This project analyzes the effectiveness and financial impact of a diabetes awareness campaign conducted across multiple cities. The objective of the analysis is to evaluate how healthcare awareness initiatives influence disease screening, diagnosis, treatment adoption, and potential pharmaceutical revenue generation.

The project demonstrates how data analytics can be applied to measure the business and healthcare outcomes of awareness campaigns in the pharmaceutical industry.

Project Objectives

Analyze healthcare awareness campaign reach and engagement

Evaluate screening and diagnosis conversion rates

Measure treatment adoption from diagnosed patients

Estimate pharmaceutical revenue generated from treated patients

Calculate return on investment (ROI) for the awareness campaign

Visualize campaign performance using an Excel dashboard

Dataset Description

A simulated dataset covering 20 cities was created for the analysis. The dataset includes the following variables:

City

People Reached

People Screened

Diagnosed Patients

Patients Starting Treatment

Campaign Cost

This dataset allows analysis of campaign performance across different geographic regions.

Analytical Approach

The analysis follows a structured approach:

1. Campaign Reach Analysis

Evaluating how many people were exposed to the awareness campaign across different cities.

2. Healthcare Conversion Funnel

Tracking the conversion process from:

People Reached → Screened → Diagnosed → Treated

This helps measure the effectiveness of the campaign in identifying and treating diabetes patients.

3. Revenue Estimation Model

A revenue model was developed based on an assumed average monthly diabetes treatment cost. Annual revenue per patient was estimated and multiplied by the number of treated patients.

4. ROI Calculation

Campaign investment was compared with estimated revenue generated from treated patients to evaluate the financial impact of the campaign.

Dashboard Visualizations

The Excel dashboard includes the following charts:

City-wise People Reached

Campaign Cost per City

Healthcare Conversion Funnel

Screened vs Diagnosed vs Treated Analysis

Revenue vs Campaign Cost Comparison

These visualizations help identify campaign performance patterns across different cities.

Key Insights

Healthcare awareness campaigns can significantly improve disease screening rates.

Increased screening leads to higher diagnosis and treatment adoption.

Awareness campaigns can generate long-term pharmaceutical revenue through new patient treatments.

Data-driven analysis helps evaluate the effectiveness and financial viability of public health initiatives.
